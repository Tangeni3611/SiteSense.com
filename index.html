F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

It depends on the project scope. A basic website usually takes 1–2 weeks, while more complex sites can take 3–6 weeks. We’ll give you a detailed timeline during our consultation.

Can I update the website content myself?

Yes! We build user-friendly websites using CMS platforms like WordPress or custom admin panels, so you can easily make updates without coding knowledge.

Do you offer website maintenance services?

Absolutely. We offer monthly maintenance plans that include updates, backups, performance checks, and security monitoring to keep your site running smoothly.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes, all our websites are fully responsive, meaning they look and work great on phones, tablets, and desktops.

Can you help me with domain and hosting?

Yes. We can help you register a domain, choose the best hosting plan, and set everything up so you're ready to launch.

How much does a website cost?

The cost depends on your needs. We offer affordable packages for startups and more advanced solutions for growing businesses. Contact us for a custom quote.
